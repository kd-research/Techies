Instructions

Math Dash is a fast-paced endless runner where you overcome obstacles by solving math problems.

Objective:
- Run as far as you can while solving math problems to clear obstacles.
- Collect numbers and power-ups to boost your score.

Controls:
- Swipe Left: Move to the left lane
- Swipe Right: Move to the right lane
- Swipe Up: Jump over obstacles
- Swipe Down: Slide under obstacles

Math Obstacles:
- Choose the correct lane that corresponds to the correct answer to the displayed equation.

Scoring:
- Correct Answer: +10 points.
- Wrong Answer: -5 points.

Power-ups:
- Magnet: Attracts nearby collectibles.
- Shield: Protects from one obstacle collision.
- Double Points: Temporarily doubles score earnings.
